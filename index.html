Fun Facts About Cheetahs

The cheetah is a large cat native to Africa and central Iran.

4 facts about cheetahs:

  1. The fastest land animal in the world, a cheetah can reach 112km/h in just three seconds – that’s faster than a sports car accelerates!
  2. Cheetahs have a pale yellow coat with black dots on the upper parts, and are white on the underbelly. Their faces are distinguished by prominent, black lines that curve from the inner corner of each eye to the outer corners of the mouth.
  3. Cheetahs are carnivores and live off other animals they find on Africa’s plains
  4. These fierce felines hunt during the day to avoid competition from other powerful predators such as lions, hyenas and leopards.
